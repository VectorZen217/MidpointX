# Logic Shift: THEO_FS_01
Trace ID: PROACTIVE-travel-concierge-workflow-1777651200028
Learned At: 2026-05-01T16:01:00.413Z

## Justification
The standard approach of manually writing a README.md from scratch can lead to inconsistent documentation and may miss crucial sections. Pre-populating with a structured template ensures all necessary aspects of a project's design are considered from the outset, improving clarity and maintainability.

## Discovered Pattern
Creating a README.md for a new project's design document.

## Optimized Approach
When creating a README.md for a new project's design document, pre-populate it with a structured template that includes sections for Introduction, Assumptions, Proposed Workflow, Technical Stack, Configuration, and Future Enhancements. This ensures a consistent and comprehensive starting point for architectural documentation.
