# US Senator Background Sources

For detailed biographical and historical information, use these sources:

## 1. Biographical Directory of the US Congress (BioGuide)
- **URL:** [bioguide.congress.gov](https://bioguide.congress.gov/)
- **Use for:** Official chronological summary of career since 1789.

## 2. Ballotpedia
- **URL:** [ballotpedia.org](https://ballotpedia.org/)
- **Use for:** Detailed candidate connection surveys, previous offices held (state/local), and political background.

## 3. OpenSecrets
- **URL:** [opensecrets.org](https://www.opensecrets.org/)
- **Use for:** "Revolving Door" information (previous lobbying, corporate boards) and industry connections.

## 4. Vital Statistics on Congress (Brookings)
- **URL:** [brookings.edu](https://www.brookings.edu/vital-statistics-on-congress/)
- **Use for:** Occupational backgrounds and historical trends.
