# Senator Email Template

Use this template as a baseline for communicating with a Senator's office.

---

**Subject:** [Bill Number/Topic] - Constituent Inquiry

**Dear Senator [Senator's Last Name],**

My name is [Your Name], and I am a constituent from [Your City, State]. I am writing to you today regarding [specific bill number or issue].

[Briefly state your position or question here. Be specific and concise.]

[Optional: Share a personal story or specific data point that illustrates why this issue is important to your community.]

I would appreciate a response detailing your position on this matter and how you plan to vote when it comes to the Senate floor.

Thank you for your time and for your service to our state.

**Sincerely,**

[Your Full Name]
[Your Address]
[Your Phone Number]
[Your Email Address]
